# Viral QA Checklist

This checklist is enforced by the generator and should be used to verify every script is viral-ready.

## Hook (Overlay Text)
- Hook headline + subtitle must match an entry in `hook_lines.md` exactly.
- Hook must use a proven format (examples):
  - “How to…”, “Watch this”, “pt 1/pt.1”, “let X cook”, “plan b”, “take notes”.
- Hook should promise value or drama (no flat descriptions).

## Visual Parity
- DM UI matches viral references (blue outgoing bubble, “You replied to their story” header).
- No dead frames: every visual includes DM context or hook context.
- Story thumbnail stays prominent (do not shrink).

## Pushback Opener (First Girl Line)
- Must be short confusion/pushback (≤4 words or “???”).
- Must include “?” or “??”.

## Reveal (Boy Line Right After Pushback)
- Must be a punchline (yes‑flip style, not continuation).
- Must be from the curated reveal set (e.g., “so it’s me then?”, “so you picked me?”).
- Reveal timing target: ~4–7s.

## Stinger Placement
- Must be deterministic: triggered after first pushback and before reveal.
- Never random.

## Mid Banter
- Every boy line is punchy/escalating (no filler).
- Girl lines are short, skeptical, teasing (≤8 words).
- Emojis/confusion reactions allowed (e.g., “???” “😭😭”).

## Ask (Boy, Second-to-Last Line)
- Must be explicit: “text me”, “number”, or clear drinks/date invite.
- No vague/soft asks.

## Win (Girl Final Line)
- Must be clear win: “text me”, “number”, or “when/where”.
- Clean and short (≤7 words).

## Pacing
- Total length: 17–24s.
- Reveal ~4–7s; win ~12–18s.

## No Repeats
- No repeated punchlines across scripts in the same batch.
- No repeated girl reactions within the same script.

## Human Text Vibe
- Short, casual, slang‑friendly.
- Avoid formal/robotic phrasing.
